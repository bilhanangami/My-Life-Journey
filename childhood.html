My Childhood

Growing up in Kitale, Kenya - The experiences that shaped my personality

Early Years in Kitale

I was born and raised in Kitale, a town in Trans-Nzoia County known for its agricultural richness and beautiful landscapes. My childhood was filled with exploration, family bonds, and learning experiences that have significantly influenced who I am today.

Our home was always filled with love and laughter. As the first child in a family of three, I learned the importance of compromise, communication, and looking out for one another from an early age.

Family & Formative Experiences

My parents emphasized education, hard work, and community involvement. My father, an education officer, taught me about the importance of education, while my mother, a teacher, instilled in me a love for learning.

Weekends were often spent visiting our grandparents' farm, where I developed an appreciation for nature and sustainable practices. These visits taught me about food production, animal care, and the importance of agriculture to our nation's economy.
